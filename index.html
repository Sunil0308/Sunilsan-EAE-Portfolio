About me

I am a Secondary 4 student studying at Bukit View Secondary School. My hobbies are reading manga, playing volleyball with my friends and watching anime. I was a Student Councilor and CCA Exco (NCC Land) before I stepped down earlier this year. I wanted to do this project so that I could prove to myself that I love coding and I wouldn't find it tiring even if faced with hurdles. After doing this project, I realized that I find coding very exciting, frustrating at times, and educational. It's a simple website, nothing fancy, but it means a lot as it took me days and countless youtube videos to help me code this website.

I want to join this course as although I didn't take computing as a subject, I wanted to learn coding and the how AI works. As learning code from youtube can only get you so far, I would like to join the course to answer my questions of major concepts like adding functions to code, or making a responsive page that works as you interact with the website or page. Also, I have always had the lifelong goal of coding something that is as special as ChatGPT which has now become a part of our everyday lives. Although I may not be able to replicate the idea of a successful interactive model like chatgpt, I aspire to do something along those lines. Essentially, I want to code something that will be complex enough for me to take pride in knowing that it was something I did while also helping to give back to society which has helped me broaden my horizons.
